About Us

Cafe Milan is a family owned and was operated on 2021 which provides a on-the-go coffee experience which goal is to give Bacolodnons an authentic coffee that is not as expensive as the big chains, but at the same time top of its class.

The business advocacy is to support local and reducing waste. Cafe Milan only sources its products only from local coffee farmers and most of the supplies are from businesses around Bacolod. It also support strawless cups and often suggest reusable glass for dine in customers.
